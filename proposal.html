Custom Action Plan / Readout Memo

A short, focused memo: "Here's what your data says, and here's what we'd do about it in the next 60 days." Includes the 1–2 workflows ranked highest for an AI pilot, draft governance principles based on what your team flagged, and recommended next-phase scope.

In-room, phones away. Demystifies what AI is and isn't, walks through CHPL's survey results live, surfaces governance questions, and lands shared vocabulary for the leadership team. Format: 30 min foundations, 30 min CHPL-specific data, 30 min discussion.

Funding option

Ohio Tech Credit (if it returns in July)

Ohio's TechCred program reimburses employers up to $2,000 per W2 employee for upskilling in approved tech areas, including AI training. The program paused but is widely expected to reopen in July 2026.

For Phase 1 specifically, this would offset RUDI's invoice if Phase 1 staff training qualifies. For Phase 2 (manager track, 65 staff), the math is much more significant. We're happy to structure the engagement so it stays eligible for retroactive credit if/when the program reopens.
